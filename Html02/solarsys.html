WORKSHOPS

Workshops are a wonderful way for people to learn and work together in a more personal setting. We have taught workshops across the United States as part of a workshop tour, as well as on college campuses, for professional associations, and for business groups. Our workshops range from woodcarving, digital and analog illustration, to building a business and getting inspired toward professional goals. Workshops can be customized based on a group’s needs.
